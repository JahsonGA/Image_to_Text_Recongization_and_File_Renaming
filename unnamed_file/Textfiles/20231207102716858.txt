MAY 1972

NEW FACES--Two new men, Robert P. Hunter and Lawrence S. Cote,
recently joined Continuing Education.

Mr, Hunter is now a field specialist with PENNTAP. He's a Penn
State graduate with bachelor and master of science degrees in
electrical engineering. A staff engineer with HRB-Singer, Inc. ’
from 1949 to 1970, Mr. Hunter comes to PENNTAP from Ordnance
Research Laboratory, where he was manager of program development.
Mr. Cote was recently appointed area representative at Allentown,
A recent Penn State graduate with a bachelor of arts degree in
speech, Mr. Cote served three years in the U. S, Army, including
a tour of duty in Vietnam.
