NEW MEN--William C. Arble has joined the PENNTAP staff as a field
specialist, and two new men, John W. Hamilton and Lowell G. Rein, are
how on the Management Development faculty.

Mr. Arble will travel extensively for PENNTAP, aiding businesses,
industries, municipalities, health organizations, government agencies,
civic groups, and individual entrepreneurs, all of whom will benefit
from his knowledge of business and of fire and safety equipment.

Mr, Arble received his bachelor's and master's degrees in agricultural
engineering from Michigan State University and Penn State, respectively
He served for nine years on the Penn State faculty, then left the
University to form William C. Arble & Associates and was later owner

of Fire Safety Equipment Company in State College.
