PENNTAP - Technical Assistance for Pennsylvania Industry

Sponsored by the Governor's Seience Advisory Committee and the
Pennsylvania Department of Commerce, the Pennsylvania Technical
Assistance Program (PENNTAP) is a research instrument available to
Pennsylvania industries, municipalities, community organizations,
governmental organizations, and others seeking to solve problems of
a scientific or technical nature. This is a free service available
through the twenty-three Pennsylvania State University Continuing
Education offices.

The object of PENNTAP is to buila a statewide network for dispersal
of technical information, reduce duplication of research, and assist

Pennsylvania groups in getting a better return on their tax dollar.
